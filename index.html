Nourish Network

End Food Waste!

Every year, millions of pounds of food are wasted and many people go hungry. Nourish Network aims to address this issue by providing a platform for events and restaurants to donate their excess food to NGOs near their location. Help us reduce food waste and feed those in need.

OUR MISSION

At Nourish Network, our mission is to end food waste and hunger. We believe that by connecting events and restaurants with NGOs, we can help redistribute excess food to those in need. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable and equitable food system for everyone.
